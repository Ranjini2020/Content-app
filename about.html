About Us

SLT MN Guiding Academy gives students the flexibility to learn at home with a curriculum that meets rigorous international standards.

We believe that each and every student has unique abilities, and all students perform better when they receive individual attention in a conducive learning environment.

Our teaching approach is student-centered and we have passionate, caring and talented team who are trained to handle online teaching.

Our Mission

The mission of SLT MN Guiding Academy is to assist and guide marginalised community children, completing their high school and earning high school diploma. We believe that it's very important for children to receive schooling, irrespective of their staus in host countries, because it creates sense of security and hope, which is often lacking in their settings.

Our program helps students build on their individual strengths and interests in a setting that is both safe and connected to a larger community.
